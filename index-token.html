About

$INDEX: the native token of Index Coop

Index Coop is a decentralized, autonomous asset manager that issues its own native token, $INDEX.

Use your $INDEX to govern Index Coop, by voting for and against proposals on Snapshot. Or buy and hold $INDEX as a long-term investment, because each $INDEX token represents a share of Index Coop’s future revenues and success.

You can earn $INDEX rewards by contributing to the growth and sustainability of Index Coop, or by providing liquidity for Index Coop products on decentralized exchanges. You can also just buy $INDEX on a centralized or decentralized exchange.

INDEX is an ERC-20 token deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. It has a capped maximum supply of 10,000,000 tokens. INDEX is used to vote in changes to the Index Coop including but not limited to:

What are the tokenomics of $INDEX?

$INDEX has a supply cap of 10,000,000 units that vest gradually over three years. Of the total supply, 70% was set aside for the community, liquidity providers and methodologists as follows:

1% was initially distributed to past holders and liquidity providers of $DPI
9% have been allocated to a liquidity mining program that spanned 60 days from launch of Index Coop
7.5% have been allocated to the Index Methodology Bounty, to be rewarded to Methodologists over 18 months (time period ends in June 2022)
52.5% have been allocated to the community treasury, to be vested over a period of 3 years

The remaining 30% have been allocated to the two parties involved in the launch of Index Coop: DeFi Pulse (2%) and Set Protocol (28%).

How can I earn $INDEX?

If you don’t want to buy $INDEX, you can earn token rewards by contributing valuable work to Index Coop.
‍
The Coop compensates contributors through treasury rewards as well as bounties and grants. Read about becoming a contributor here.

Methodologists can earn $INDEX via the ‘Index Methodology Bounty’.

Learn more about what’s needed to become a Methodologist here. And finally, liquidity providers can earn $INDEX by staking their LP tokens - learn how to do so here.

Where can I buy $INDEX?

You can buy $INDEX on many DEXs. Uniswap and Sushiswap are good places to start, as is Balancer.

$INDEX as a governance token

Voting and governance with $INDEX 

As a governance token, the primary utility of $INDEX is its role in the governance process of the Index Coop DAO. Voting is how we, as a community, make important decisions. Each $INDEX token represents one vote; so the more $INDEX you hold, the more voting power you have. Proposals you can support or object to with your share of $INDEX might be about:
‍
Compensation for valuable contributors

Launching new products
Maintenance or distribution of existing products
Composition and use of treasury
Forming/restructuring of working groups
Compensation for valuable contributors

How do I vote? 

When there is a proposal you’d like to vote on (and ideally you vote on all of them) head over to our Vote page.

Connect your wallet, choose the proposal, vote for or against it and sign the message (no transaction costs).
‍
That’s it - welcome to the world of decentralized organizations!

Metagovernance voting with $INDEX

With $INDEX you can also vote on proposals of selected protocols underlying our products. As of mid-2021, you can help govern a subset of protocols held in the DeFi Pulse Index (DPI). This is known as metagovernance.
‍
As $DPI grows, you can expect the number of protocols where $INDEX holders can exercise metagovernance to grow along with it.

You can expect metagovernance opportunities via Metaverse Index (MVI) and other thematic index products too.

Investing in $INDEX

Should I buy $INDEX?

$INDEX is, first and foremost, a governance token.
At the same time, it accrues some value when Index Coop, as an organization, grows and succeeds.
‍
Index Coop has seen tremendous growth to date, making it the clear market leading decentralised asset manager.
‍
Only 7 months after the release of the $INDEX token, it reached an all-time high of almost $70, valuing Index Coop at over $300 million on a fully diluted basis.

Nine months after inception, Index Coop has over $221 million in TVL and a number of other products in the pipeline.

As a first mover with a very professional, skilled and highly active community, we might just have what it takes to continue our upward journey in the future. 

Are there other ways for $INDEX accrue value?

While the details of the value capture mechanism for $INDEX are not fully worked out yet, there are a number of interesting possibilities to consider.
Streaming fees from index products accruing to the token is one option.
Another would be intrinsic productivity - a scenario where Index Coop productively uses the assets held in our index products, for example for yield farming or staking.
